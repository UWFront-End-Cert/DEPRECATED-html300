History

Farmers' markets have been around for a long as we have been around, from the earliest civilizations to modern times

Markets can be found in the smallest rural area to the largest and most populated cities

And they can be found all over the globe.

The concept of a "farmers' market" can be misleading. They are not limited to products produced on farms or sold by farmers, rather some markets have almost every imaginable item for sale

In some places, these markets can take up city blocks and can be larger than an Amazon warehouse

Of course, Washington has it fair share of markets all over the state and in some communities there are several local markets

And, I am sure that the most known and world-famous Washington market can be found in Seattle. It is known as the Pike Place Market
