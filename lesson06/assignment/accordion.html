Your Name

A teenage boy and girl embark on a quest to meet each other for the
first time after they magically swap bodies.
Release date: April 7, 2017 (USA)
Director: Makoto Shinkai
Japanese: 君の名は。
Featured song: Zenzenzense
Did you know: "Your Name" is the second-highest-grossing anime film of
all time worldwide ($361,024,012). wikipedia.org

Weathering with You

Weathering with You is a Japanese animated film written and directed by
Makoto Shinkai and produced by CoMix Wave Films, following the 2016
film Your Name. It was released in Japan on July 19, 2019, starring
Kotaro Daigo and Nana Mori. Wikipedia
Initial release: July 19, 2019 (Japan)
Director: Makoto Shinkai
Japanese: 天気の子
Story by: Makoto Shinkai
Screenplay: Makoto Shinkai

My Neighbor Totoro

This acclaimed animated tale by director Hayao Miyazaki follows schoolgirl
Satsuke and her younger sister, Mei, as they settle into an old country house
with their father and wait for their mother to recover from an illness in an area hospital.
Release date: May 7, 1993 (USA)
Director: Hayao Miyazaki
Featured song: My Neighbor Totoro
Japanese: となりのトトロ
Cast: Shigesato Itoi, Chika Sakamoto, Sumi Shimamoto..
